AcerbE™

Forensic Watermarking & Tamper-Protection Engine

●
Core Specifications

AcerbE™ is a production-ready forensic watermarking and tamper-protection engine designed for institutional-grade IP protection. Embeds imperceptible, cryptographically-anchored fingerprints into digital assets. Available standalone or embedded in RaPaX™ for automatic point-of-sale watermarking. Every asset receives a unique identifier tied to transaction metadata and blockchain verification.

●
Features & Capabilities
Imperceptible Watermarks
Cryptographically-anchored fingerprints embedded without visible degradation to asset quality.
Tamper Detection
Detect unauthorized modification, duplication, or distribution of protected assets.
Blockchain Integration
Anchor fingerprints to Mahihkan™ proof wallet and distributed ledgers for permanent audit trail.
RaPaX™ Embedded Mode
Automatic watermarking at point of sale, seamless integration with vending workflows.
Multi-Asset Support
Protects software, digital media, documents, and proprietary data formats.
Production-Ready
Institutional-grade deployment, forensic audit logging, enterprise compliance certified.
●
Use Cases

Software Distribution: Embed unique identifiers in software binaries for license verification and piracy detection.

Digital Media Protection: Watermark artwork, music, video, and documents to track unauthorized distribution.

Institutional Compliance: Proof of authenticity and chain-of-custody for regulated financial and legal documents.

Creator IP Protection: Enable creators to claim ownership and enforce rights over digital creations.
