Crypto Hall of Alexandria™

Sovereign Zero-Trust Cryptocurrency Library & Native Land Initiative

●
Core Mission

Crypto Hall of Alexandria™ is a sovereign, zero-trust cryptocurrency education library anchored to the Mahihkan™ proof wallet. Serves as the foundation for the Native Land Initiative — a humanitarian pillar dedicated to supporting vulnerable populations through cryptocurrency education, open-source infrastructure, and financial empowerment. Planned transfer to Alētheia Foundation Inc. (federal NPO + CRA registered charity).
