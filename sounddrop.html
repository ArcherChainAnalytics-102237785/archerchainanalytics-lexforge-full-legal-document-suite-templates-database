SoundDrop™

Audio-First Short-Form Video Editor for Musicians

Core Specifications

SoundDrop™ is an audio-first short-form video editor optimized for musicians and music creators. Upload your tracks, auto-generate visuals, sync to TikTok/Instagram/YouTube Shorts, publish to multiple platforms simultaneously. AI-powered audio analysis for beat detection, vocal isolation, and mood matching to visual effects.

Audio-First Editing
Upload your track. Video follows. Auto-sync visuals to beats and drops.
Beat Detection

Visual Effects Library

Multi-Platform Publish
